Selected Work

Selected projects showing how the work can be applied

01 / Audio-Reactive Visual System · 2026

XIA (Kim Junsu) — Gravity Album Listening Session

A 30-minute audio-reactive visual system created for the official pre-release listening session for XIA’s full-length album Gravity.

Adaptable for album previews, artist showcases, launch events and music-led brand experiences.

02 / Large-Scale Media Art Visuals · 2025

PayPal — Melbourne Fashion Festival

Screen content created for the official PayPal runway at Melbourne Fashion Festival — media art visuals designed around choreography, lighting and broadcast camera lines.

Suitable for large-format screens, cultural events, festivals and spatial media environments.

03 / Interactive Installation · 2024–present

The Meta Rose Project

An interactive audio-visual installation that uses roses as tactile interfaces to trigger real-time sound and visuals.

Adaptable for brand pop-ups, interactive booths, shareable visual moments and experiential retail environments.

04 / Creative Technology Workshop

TouchCollective / Melbourne Fringe Workshop

A participatory workshop exploring TouchDesigner, conductive touch, flowers and plants, collective sound and real-time visuals.

Available as TouchDesigner workshops, team-building sessions and arts education programmes.
